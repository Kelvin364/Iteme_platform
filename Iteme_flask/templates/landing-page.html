Services We Offer

Community outreach


Community awareness efforts feature infographic sessions on mental health disorders, myth-busting articles, and personal testimonials on our hotline, fostering understanding and support for mental health challenges.

Games and fun activities include interactive quizzes on mental health, online workshops for stress management, and virtual support groups to foster connection and sharing among individuals facing mental health challenges.

The #INeedHelpInitiative provides inspirational quotes, resource guides for local mental health services, and peer support networks to encourage seeking help and provide assistance and advice, acting as a bridge to wellness.

About Us

Our story began with a vision to address the growing need for accessible mental health support within Rwandan communities. We understand the unique challenges young Rwandans face, from academic pressures to social anxieties. Iteme was created to provide a safe space where these challenges can be openly discussed and addressed
